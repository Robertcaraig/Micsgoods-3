Our Company

We are a company that provides 'biotechnological' solutions to our agricultural and water treatment problems. We are at the forefront of helping the agricultural sector whose farms are constantly attacked by pests and diseases affecting the output and production. The introduction of pesticides to address the problem was implemented until such time that the pests became resistant and the soil's toxicity level increased. Thus affecting the quality and quantity of production, the health effect on the consumers, and contaminating the water resources.

Our focus is to help reduce the inputs, increase the productivity of the farmers, uplift their livelihood and bring the price of the output to a level affordable to the consumers. This can be achieved through the implementation of new farming techniques through the introduction of bio inoculants and biofertilizers that will gradually rejuvenate the soil to its original state and bring about organically grown foods. Through the use of biotech products farm inputs are initially reduced by as much as 50% and at the same time increases the yield by 35-50% with better quality and taste.
